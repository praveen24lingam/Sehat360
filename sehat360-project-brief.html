Sehat360

Family Health Navigator for Indian families — medicines, government schemes, maternal care, child vaccination, aur health awareness — sab ek jagah.

💡

Project Description

Hackathon submission brief

India mein crores log har mahine medicines pe zaroorat se zyada paisa kharch karte hain — kyunki unhe Jan Aushadhi ke baare mein nahi pata. Woh government health schemes ke liye eligible hain, par apply nahi kar paate — kyunki koi guide nahi karta. Pregnant women checkups miss karti hain — kyunki koi tracker nahi hai. Bachche vaccinations se peeche reh jaate hain — kyunki card kho jaata hai.

Sehat360 yeh sab problems ek jagah solve karta hai. Yeh ek Progressive Web App hai jo Urban lower-middle class Indian families ke liye bana hai — especially woh mahila jo ghar ki health decisions manage karti hai.

"Sehat360 — Apni sehat ka poora hisaab, ek jagah."
